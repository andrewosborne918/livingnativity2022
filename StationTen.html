Living Nativity 2021

Station 10: The Story Goes On…

Click "play" for audio.

For those who brought a donation tonight, notice where it is placed. Our volunteers place all of these donations at the foot of this last illuminated cross. Even in our community, there are people who do not have all they need. They truly are in need of more than they have. But through Christ’s love we come together. We come together to support those in need, to encourage those hurting, and to love each and every person we encounter. This is what we get to do, because this is what Christ has done for us.

There is so much more to this story of Christ and what He does for you. If there is any way we can help you or you want to know more about anything you experienced tonight, feel free to contact us. The best way is to email: information@cornerstonelutheran.church. On our website, cornerstonelutheran.church you can see all of the things we still have going on, including online and in-person Christmas Eve Worship. Merry Christmas!
